Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the location and duration of internship?

    The Internship and training will be virtually and duration will be 1 month.

  • When we will get certificate of internship completion and LOR ?

    You will recieve your internship completion certificate and letter of recommendation upto the next month 5 working days.

  • Which type projects we have during internship ?

    You will get projects/tasks related to your internship domain and you will get three projects that you have to complete as a beginner, intermediate and advance.

  • What is eligibility criteria for Certificate and LOR ?

    If you complete all three projects then you will recieve Internship completion certificate. Interns who will perform well and follow the given instructions will recieve the Letter of Recommendation.

  • What is the fee of Internship/Training?

    Only 99Rs. will be charged at the end of internship/training for mentorship/guidance.
